About Shwe Kraoo Buddhist Temple

Founded on June 15, 1911, Shwe Kraoo Buddhist Temple has been dedicated to making a positive impact in our community for over 150 years.

Our Location

Harbang, Chakariya, Cox's Bazar

Our Founders

  • Un Kra U
  • Khaing Ching
  • Shwe Hla Fru

Our Mission

At Shwe Kraoo Buddhist Temple, we strive to promote and preserve the core values and teachings of buddhism.

Our History

2005

Shwe Kraoo Buddhist Temple is established by Un Kra U, Khaing Ja Prue and Shwe Hla Fru with a mission to promote and preserve the core values and teachings of buddhism.

2010

Expanded our services to include support for elderly community members.

2015

Opened our first community center, providing a space for education and social gatherings.

2025

Launched our Website to make globally digitally visible.
